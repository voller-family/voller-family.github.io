Fredericka Rachel Miller (Gritmacker)

1862 - 1944

House of Voller biography for Fredericka Miller (Gritmacker), 1862 - 1944. Born to Johann Grutzmacher and Sophie Gritmacker. Married to Fred Miller.

Obituary

Rachel Miller who was 81 years of age passed away at the home of her daughter, Mrs. Ernest Swanson, near Woodstockat 10:15 o'clock Wednesday., January 5, after a lingering illness. She was born in Germany March 30, 1862 and came to this country while still a young woman. She was a resident of the Union Community for about 50 years. She was married to Fred Miller who preceded her in death as did one son Henry, who died in World War I.

Survivors include two sons, Charles and William Miller o Union, seven daughters, Mrs. Anna Petitt of Dundee, Mrs. Bertha Polnow of Rio, Wis, Miss Mayne Miller of Elgin, Mrs. Ella Merritt of Kenosha, Wis, Mrs. Florence Ebert of St. Louis, Mo, Mrs. Elsie Korn of Marengo and Mrs. Edith Swanson of Woodstock, 23 grandchildren and 6 great-grandchildren.
