Charlotte Lydia Rasch

1923 - 1991

House of Voller biography for Charlotte Rasch, 1923 - 1991. Born in Sharon, Wisconsin to Jacob and Karolina Voller. Wife of Lyle Rasch.

Obituary

Charlotte L. Rasch, 67, 314 Martin Ave., Twin Lakes, died Sunday morning (April 21, 1991) at her residence.

The daughter of the late Jacob and Lena (Werthmueller) Voler, she was born Oct. 5, 1923, in Sharon.

On Oct. 1, 1941, she married Lyle J. Racsh at Peace Lutheran Church in Wilmont.

A resident of the Twin Lakes area for most of her life, she farmed in the town of Randall for many years. She also worked for the Intermatic Corp., Spring Grove, ILl., for 24 years, retiring in 1987.

She was a member of the Peace Lutheran Church and its Ladies Aid, the Quarter Century Club and the Culture Club of Intermatic, and the Randall Rustlers 4-H Club. She also loved to sew, knit, and crochet.

Surviving are two daughters, Mrs. Charles (Betty) Lackas, Twin Lakes, and Carol Hegner, Grayslake, Ill.; three brothers, Jack, Delavan, Harry, Walworth, and Carl, Marengo, Ill; four sisters, Mrs. William (Lena) Schuld, Sharon, Mrs. Harlan (Hattie) Nichols, Walworth, Olga Moede, Harvard, Ill., and Arlene Weir, Janesville; and three granddaughters, Robin and Julie Hegner and April Lackas.

Preceding her in death were her husband, Lyle, May 5, 1987, and two sisters, Bertha Miller and Rosie Sweet.
