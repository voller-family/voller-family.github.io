House of Voller biography for Ernest Beu, 1982 - 1967. Born in Huntley, Illinois to Charles and Marie Beu.

Obituary

Wednesday 31 May 1967

Ernest Beu, 74, died Tuesday morning in Valley Hi, Hartland.

A farmer in the Woodstock and Hebron area for many years, he was born Dec. 14 1892, in Huntley the son of Charles and Marie Beu.

Survivors are two brothers, Frant, McComb, and Walter, Dearborn, Mich. and a sister, Mrs. Emma Grisham, Elgin.

He was preceded in death by seven brothers.
