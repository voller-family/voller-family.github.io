Obituary

Alice Louise Voller, 93, of Marengo passed away February 16, 2022 surrounded by family.

She was born February 18, 1928 in Riley Township to Max and Elsie (Miller) Korn. On August 4, 1951 she married Carl Voller. Alice enjoyed spending time with her family, especially her grandchildren. Her hobbies included baking, sewing, and playing cards with her friends. She was a farmer at heart and loved gardening. Alice was a member of the Ladies Aid at Zion Lutheran Church for many years.

Alice is survived by her children, Ron, Deborah, Diane, and Dean (Kimberly) Voller; grandchildren, Stephanie and Peter Voller, Jacob, Jordan and Grant Voller; and her sister, Maxine Wightman. She was preceded in death by her husband, Carl; her parents; her siblings, George Abraham, Don Miller, and LeRoy Korn.
