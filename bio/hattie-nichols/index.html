Hattie M. Nichols

1919 - 2003

House of Voller biography for Hattie Nichols, 1919 - 2003. Born in Sharon, Wisconsin to Jacob and Karolina Voller. Wife of Harland Nichols.

Obituary

Hattie Nichols, 84, Walworth, died Tuesday, Nov. 25, 2003, after a long illness.

She was born May 24, 1919, in Sharon Township, the daughter of Jacob and Lena Wertmueller Voller.

She married Harland Nichols Dec. 9, 1939, in Walworth.

She was a homemaker and an avid gardener. She helped her husband with his lawn and garden sales business and welding shop. They retired in 1979, splitting time! between Walworth and Buchanan Dam, Texas.

She was a member of Faith Church, Walworth.

She is survived by one son, Dennis, Sioux Falls, S.D.; one daughter, Sandy Webb, Edgewood, N.M.; six grandchildren; three great-grandchildren; two sisters, Olga Moede, Harvard, Ill., and Arlene Weir, Janesville; and one brother, Carl, Marengo, Ill.

She was preceded in death by her husband; three sisters, Bertha Miller, Rosie Sweet and Charlotte Rasch; and two brothers. Jack, and Harry. Her other sister, Lena Schuld, died Wednesday, Nov. 26, 2003.
