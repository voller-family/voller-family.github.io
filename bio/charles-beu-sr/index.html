Charles J. Beu

1853 - 1923

House of Voller biography for Charles Beu, 1853 - 1923. Born in Germany. Married to Marie Beu.

Obituary

Charles Beu, who had been suffering from Bright's disease for the past three months, passed away at his home Wednesday, March 28.

The funeral was held from the Lutheran Church, of which he was a member for many years. Besides the widow he leaves eleven children to mourn his loss: Fred, Ernest and Will of Huntley, Henry of Dundee, Louis of Dayton, Ohio, John, Charley, and George of Woodstock, Frant of Hebron, Walter of Evanston and Mrs. Emma Grisham of Elgin.
