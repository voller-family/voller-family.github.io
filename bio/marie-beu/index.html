Marie Johanna Fredericka Christine Beu (Gritmacker)

1855 - 1926

House of Voller biography for Marie Beu (Gritmacker), 1855 - 1926. Born in Germany to Johann Grutzmacher and Sophie Gritmacker. Married to Charles Beu.

Obituary

Mrs. Charles Beu, 71 years of age died at her home at Huntley, Wednesday morning, after an illness of seven months.

The early part of her life was spent at Hampshire and for the last sixty years she lived at Huntley, Ill.

She is survived by one daughter, Mrs. J.B. Grisham, of Elgin, Ill and ten sons: George and Charles of Woodstock; Ernest, William and Fred of Huntley; Louis of Dayton, Ohio; Henry of Dundee, Ill, John of Elgin, Ill; Walter of Evanston, Ill and Frank of Hebron, Ill.
