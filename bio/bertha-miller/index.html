Bertha M. Miller

1910 - 1988

House of Voller biography for Bertha Miller, 1910 - 1988. Born in Sharon, Wisconsin to Jacob and Karolina Voller. Wife of Edwin Miller.

Obituary

Miller, 77, died at home, Tuesday, March 29, 1988.

Born in Sharon Township, Oct. 26, 1910, she was the daughter of the late Jacob and Karolina (Werthmueller) Voller.

On Feb. 14, 1929, in Harvard, she married Edwin Miller. He died Aug. 14, 1978. After their marriage they lived five years in Harvard, 15 years in Alden and the past 40 years in Sharon

For 20 years she had worked at Oaks Manufacturing, Elkhorn, Wis., and four years at Val-O-Will Farms, Lake Geneva, Wis.

She belonged to Trinity Lutheran Church, Harvard, the Sharon Homemakers, the Sharon Senior Citizens and Sharon Telecare.

Mrs. Miller was the mother of five sons, Kenneth (Dorothy) Miller, Lake Geneva, Leonard (Judy) Miller and David (Pam) Miller both of Sharon, Russell (Mildred) Miller, Twin Lakes, Wis. She had 23 grandchildren; three stepgrandchildren; 16 great-grandchildren; three brothers, Carl Voller, Marengo, Harry Voller, Walworth, Wis., and Jack Voler, Delavan, Wis., and five sisters, Lena Schuld, Sharon, Harrie Nichols, Walworth, Olga Moede, Harvard, Arlene Weir, Janesville, and Charlotte Rasch, Twin Lakes.

Two great-grandchildren and a sister, Rose Sweet, preceded her in death.
