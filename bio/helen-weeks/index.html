Helen M. Weeks (Cates)

1927 - 2014

House of Voller biography for Helen Weeks (Cates), 1927 - 2014. Born in Hebron, Illinois. Married to Harold Weeks. Parent to Charles.

Obituary

Helen M. Weeks, age 87, of Sharon, WI passed away Saturday October 11, 2014, at Beloit Memorial Hospital in Beloit, WI

She was born March 17, 1927, in Hebron, IL, the daughter of Charles & Adeline (Mason) Cates. She married Harold Weeks October 2, 1946, in Hebron, IL

Helen was a member of the Sharon United Methodist Church, Stateline Country Club and an avid bowler. Helen was a devoted wife, mother, grandmother, great grandmother and friend to many. Her greatest joy was cooking for her boys and anyone else who showed up at the kitchen table. She was famous for her baking and especially for her angel food cake.

She is survived by her loving husband of 68 years, Harold; four sons, Chuck of Sharon, WI, Dave (Judie) of Walworth, WI, Bill (Betty) of Surprise, AZ & Don (Debbie) of Sharon; ten grandchildren; ten great grandchildren; a sister, Charlene Schultz of Harvard, IL; a brother, John (JoAnn) Cates of Elkhorn, WI; many other loving relatives and friends.

She was preceded by her parents; a sister, Betty Oberst; a brother, Ralph Cates; and a daughter-in-law, Lois Weeks.

Special thanks to Dr. Leo & the staff of Beloit Regional Hospice, Beloit Memorial Hospital and Golden Years Retirement Center and especially Jenny Kirkpatrick for special care shown to Helen these past few weeks.
