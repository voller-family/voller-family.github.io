Fred Miller

1854 - 1937

House of Voller biography for Fred Miller, 1854 - 1937. Born in Germany to Frederick and Marie Miller. Married to Fredericka Miller.

Obituary

Fred Miller was born November 10, 1854 in Germany. He died February 26, 1937 after a four-month illness in his home in Union.

He leaves to mourn, his widow, the former Rachel Gritmacker, nine children, 23 grandchildren, and three great-grandchildren. One son died in World War I.
