Max Korn

1890 - 1979

House of Voller biography for Max Korn, 1890 - 1979. Born in Union, Illinois to Richard and Otelia Korn. Married to Elsie Korn.

Obituary

Max Korn, age 89, of 418 South State Street, Marengo, died at the Florence Nursing Home Monday Evening.

He was born near Union on July 7, 1890, the son of Richard and Otelia Korn. He farmed around the Huntley-Union area for many years.

He was a member of the Trinity Lutheran Church of Huntley.

Survivors include two daughters, Alice Voller of Marengo and Maxine Wightman of Littleton, Colorado; two sons, LeRoy of Villa Park, IL and Donald Miller of Corvallis, Oregon; 12 grandchildren and 10 great-grandchildren.
