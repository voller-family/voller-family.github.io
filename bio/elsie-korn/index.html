Elsie Catherine Korn (Miller)

1895 - 1977

House of Voller biography for Elsie Korn (Miller), 1895 - 1977. Born in Union, Illinois to Fred and Fredericka Miller.

Obituary

Mrs. Elsie Korn, 82, 419 S. State St., Marengo, died Thursday, June 23, in St. Joseph Hospital, Belvidere.

Born Feb. 10, 1895, in Union City, Ill, she was the daughter of Fred and Rachel Gritmacher Miller. She was a member of the Ladies Aid of the Trinity Lutheran Church in Huntly.

Surviving are her husband, Max. Korn; two sons, Donald Abraham of Corvallis, Ore., and LeRoy Korn of Villa Park; two daughters, Mrs. Alice Voller of Marengo, and Mrs. Maxine Wightman of Littleton, Colo.; 12 grandchildren nine great-grandchildren; one brother, WilliamMiller of Union; and one sister, Mrs. Ella Merritt of Kenosha, Wisc.

She was preceded in death by her parents, her first husband, Max Abraham, a son, George Abraham, two brothers, and five sisters.
