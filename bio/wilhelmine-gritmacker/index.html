Wilhelmine Carolyn Gritmacker (Voss)

"Minnie"

House of Voller biography for Minnie Gritmacker (Voss), 1866 - 1947. Born in Barrington, Illinois to Carl and Henriette Voss.

Obituary

Mrs. Minnie Gritmacker of Dundee, 80, died at 5:30 this morning at the Carroll Rest Home in Elgin, following a lingering illness.

She was born February 16, 1866 in Barrington. On March 2, 1883 was married to Fred Gritmacker, who died nearly two years ago. Survivors are seven sons, Charles and Hendry of Canby, Minn.m August of Algonquin, Fred of Barrington, George of South Elgin, John of Dundee, Albert of Palatine, two daughters, Mrs. Frank Roth of Woodstock and Mrs. Wiliam Horn of Dundee, 32 grandchildren and 25 great grandchildren.
