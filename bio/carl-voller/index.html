Carl William Voller

1926 - 2012

House of Voller biography for Carl Voller, 1926 - 2012. Born in Sharon, Wisconsin to Jacob and Karolina Voller. Husband of Alice Voller.

Obituary

Carl Voller, 86, of Marengo died Sunday, July 22, 2012 at Florence Nursing Home.

He was born April 23, 1926 in Sharon, Wi., to Jacob and Lena (Worthmueller) Voller. He married Alice Korn on August 4, 1951 in Marengo. He was a truck driver for TC Industries for 45 years. He enjoyed making games, small puzzles, bird houses and feeders. He is survived by his wife of 60 years Alice, his children; Ron (Bonnie), Dean (Kimberly), Deborah and Diane Voller, grandchildren; Stephanie and Peter Voller, Jacob, Jordan and Grant Voller, and a sister Arlene Weir.

He was preceded in death by his parents, brothers Jack and Harry, and sisters; Lena Schuld, Bertha Miller, Rose Sweet, Hattie Nichols, Olga Moede and Charlotte Rasch.
