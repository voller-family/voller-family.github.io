Peanut Butter Cookies

From the kitchen of Alice Voller

Ingredients

  1. 1/2 cups1/2 cupsPeanut butter
  2. 1/4 cups1/4 cupsShortening
  3. 1/2 cup1/2 cupBrown sugar
  4. 1/2 cup1/2 cupGranulated sugar
  5. 11Egg
  6. 1 cup1 cupSifted flour
  7. 1 teaspoon1 teaspoonBaking soda

Steps

  1. Cream peanut butter and shortening together.
  2. Add sugar dradually, continuing until mixture is light and fluffy.
  3. Add beaten egg.
  4. Sift flour and baking soda together, add, mix well.
  5. Drop by teaspoon on cookie sheet.
  6. Press down with tines of a fork.
  7. Base 10-15 minutes at 350 degrees.
