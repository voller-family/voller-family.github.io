Carrot Pudding

From the kitchen of Kimberly Voller

Ingredients

  1. 1.25 cup1.25 cupCrisco
  2. 0.75 cup0.75 cupBrown Sugar
  3. 11Egg
  4. 1 teaspoon1 teaspoonbaking powder
  5. 0.5 teaspoon0.5 teaspoonbaking soda
  6. 0.5 teaspoon0.5 teaspoonSalt
  7. 1 teaspoon1 teaspoonCinnamon
  8. 0.25 teaspoon0.25 teaspoonNutmeg
  9. 1.25 cups1.25 cupsFlour
  10. 1.5 cups1.5 cupsGrated carrots

Steps

  1. Peel and grate carrots.
  2. Mix crisco, egg, and dry ingredients well in a large bowl.
  3. Slowly mix in carrots and flour until all ingredients are well mixed.
  4. Bake at 350 for 60 minutes.
