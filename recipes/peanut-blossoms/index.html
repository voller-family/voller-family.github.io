Peanut Blossoms

From the kitchen of Alice Voller

Ingredients

  1. 1 & 3/4 cups1 & 3/4 cupsFlour
  2. 1 teaspoon1 teaspoonBaking Soda
  3. 1/2 teaspoon1/2 teaspoonSalt
  4. 1/2 cup1/2 cupButter
  5. 1/2 cup1/2 cupPeanut butter
  6. 1/2 cup1/2 cupSugar
  7. 1/2 cup1/2 cupPacked brown sugar
  8. 11Egg
  9. 1 teaspoon1 teaspoonVanilla

Steps

  1. Sift together flour, baking soda, and salt.
  2. Cream butter, peanut butter, sugar, and brown sugar. Cream well.
  3. Add egg and vanilla. Beat well.
  4. Blend in the dry ingredients gradually.
  5. Shape dough into balls using a rounded teaspoon for each.
  6. Roll balls in sugar and place on ungreased cookie sheet.
  7. Bake at 375 degrees for 8 minutes.
  8. Remove sheet from oven and place a solid candy kiss on the top of each cookie, pressing down firmly so that the cookie cracks around the edge.
  9. Return cookies to oven and bake 2 to 5 minutes longer until golden brown.
