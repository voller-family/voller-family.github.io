Pumpkin Bread

From the kitchen of Alice Voller

Ingredients

  1. 3 & 1/3 cup3 & 1/3 cupAll purpose flour
  2. 1/2 teaspoon1/2 teaspoonBaking powder
  3. 2 teaspoons2 teaspoonsBaking soda
  4. 1.5 teaspoons1.5 teaspoonsSalt
  5. 1 teaspoon1 teaspoonCinnamon
  6. 1/2 Teaspoon1/2 TeaspoonCloves
  7. 2/3 Teaspoon2/3 TeaspoonNutmeg
  8. 2 & 2/3 cups2 & 2/3 cupsSugar
  9. 2/3 cups2/3 cupsShortening
  10. 44Eggs
  11. 2 Cups2 CupsCooked pumpkin
  12. 2/3 cups2/3 cupsWater

Steps

  1. Sift flour, add baking powder, soda, salt, and spices.
  2. Mix sugar and oil in a large bowl.
  3. Set mixer at low speed and add eggs one at a time.
  4. Add pumpkin.
  5. Add flour mixture and add water. Beat until mixed.
  6. Pour into two greased 3.5 x 7.5 x 3" loaf pans.
  7. Base for 1 hour at 350 degrees.
