Apple Crisp

From the kitchen of Kimberly Voller

Ingredients

  1. 10 cups10 cupsApples
  2. 1 cup1 cupSugar
  3. 1 tablespoon1 tablespoonAll-purpose flour
  4. 1 teaspoon1 teaspoonGround cinnamon
  5. 1/2 cup1/2 cupWater
  6. 1 cup1 cupQuick-cooking oats
  7. 1 cups1 cupsAll-purpose flour
  8. 1 cup1 cupPacked brown sugar
  9. 0.25 teaspoon0.25 teaspoonBaking powder
  10. 0.25 teaspoon0.25 teaspoonBaking soda
  11. 1/2 cup1/2 cupButter

Steps

  1. Thinly slice all apples.
  2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  3. Evenly spread out the sliced apples in a 9x13 inch pan.
  4. Mix the white sugar, 1 tablespoon flour, and ground cinnamon together, and sprinkle over the apples.
  5. Pour water evenly all over.
  6. Melt the butter.
  7. Combine the oats, 1 cup flour, brown sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and melted butter together. Crumble evenly over the apple mixture
  8. Bake at 350 for 45 minutes.
